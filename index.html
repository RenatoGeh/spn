Automatic Learning of Sum-Product Networks

Institute of Mathematics and Statistics, University of São Paulo

Renato Lui Geh, Supervisor: Denis Deratani Mauá


Sum-Product Networks are a new class of tractable Probabilistic Graphical Models that can compute inference in time linear to its edges. It was proposed in 2011 by Hoifung Poon and Pedro Domingos on their paper Sum-Product Networks: A New Deep Architecture [PD2011] and has shown great promise in probabilistic learning.

This undergraduate research project's objective is to not only understand what Sum-Product Networks are and how to perform inference and learning on them, but also log what I have learned in a step-by-step form, allowing others to learn about Sum-Product Networks in a much easier way.
